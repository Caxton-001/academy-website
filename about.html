About Dev Spark Academy

We believe in the power of technology to transform lives — and we are passionate about equipping the next generation with the skills to make that transformation happen.

Our Story

Founded in 2023,the Academy has quickly established itself as a top destination for problem solving through technology. What started as a small passion project has grown into a thriving community of code lovers, developers, software engineers, young people and enthusiasts.

Our mission is simple:to inspire and prepare young minds to think critically, build creatively, and solve real-world problems with technology. We believe coding is more than just writing software — it is about unlocking potential, sparking innovation, and shaping futures.

Why Choose Us

We're committed to excellence in every aspect of our undertakings.

Real world practical skills

We do not just teach theory — we focus on hands-on, practical learning that mirrors real-world industry standards.This approach ensures they leave not just with knowledge, but with the confidence and experience to create, innovate, and apply their skills meaningfully beyond the classroom.

Unmatched Quality

We are committed to delivering top-tier, industry-relevant education through carefully designed sessions led by experienced software engineers. Our curriculum is practical, modern, and aligned with the technologies shaping today’s world — ensuring our students gain skills that truly matter.

Community Focus

At the Academy, we believe learning thrives in a supportive and inspiring community. We create a space where students, mentors, and peers collaborate, share ideas, and grow together. Our partnership with schools like St. Mary’s College Kisubi allows us to nurture talent in an environment rooted in encouragement, connection, and shared purpose.
